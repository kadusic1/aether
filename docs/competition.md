# Competitive Analysis: Top AI video generator platforms

## InVideo AI

**URL:** https://invideo.io/

**Target Audience:** Faceless channel creators, social media marketers, small businesses.

**Workflow:**
1. Enter a text prompt or topic (e.g., "Top 5 dark psychology facts")
2. Preprocessing phase
3. Customize video options
    * Creative strategy (3 options)
    * Duration (45s, 1min, 2min)
    * Platform (IG Reels, TikTok, YT)
    * Media tier (Basic - stock images, Basic efficient generative models, Pro - most advanced generative models)
4. Draft video is generated (2–5 min wait)
4. Edit via "Magic Box" - conversational text commands (e.g., "change scene 3 to a city skyline")
5. Export and share/schedule

**Ease of Use:** Can feel complex with many options to choose from.

**Feature Set:**
- Full video generation from prompt + choosing video options
- 16M+ stock media assets (iStock + Storyblocks)
- AI voiceover in 50+ languages with voice cloning (paid)
- Integrates Google VEO 3.1 and Sora 2 (as of late 2025) on higher tiers
- Mobile app, real-time collaboration
- Social scheduling built-in
- Different workflows available, e.g. short video and ads
- Avatars available (including clone yourself)
- Select product from Amazon or similar platform and create an ad for it
- Manual and prompt based video editing available

**Summary:** usage of stock images/videos, real-time collaboration, social scheduling


## Lumen5

**URL:** https://lumen5.com

**Target Audience:** Social media managers, marketers and content-creators.

**Workflow (3 options):**
1. Create multiple clips option
    * Upload video, audio or select already uploaded footage with clear speech to
    generate multiple clips.
    * Choose clip format (e.g. 1:1 or 9:16), describe the clips you want (optional),
    clip length (auto, <60s, 60-90s, >90s), caption style (none, classic, minimal,
    banner)
    * Format (e.g. change to landscape), crop or trim 5 generated clips
    * Save generated clips
2. Create new video option
    * Choose a template (e.g. Astra), choose its color palette and format (e.g. 16:9)
    * Choose how to get started (AI Voiceover, Text on media or AI Avatar)
    * Choose content to turn into a video. Options present:
        - *Outline an idea* (what will your video be about e.g. Recap of Acme's leadership conference, what to reference in your script e.g. Visionary summit was a hug success),
        - *Paste text* (paste or write text you want to be converted into a video
        script)
        - *Import a URL* (paste an article or blog to be converted to a video)
        - *Upload a document* (document to be turned into a video)
    * Video generated (e.g. after taking a blog about GoLang concurrency as input
    we get a one minute video with stock images)
3. Chat to video option
    * Prompt for video creation (e.g. dark psychology facts video)
    * Video with stock images/videos and  with/without AI voiceover generated
    * Option to edit video by changing stock images, music, style (colors),
    format, AI media (requires credits e.g. Nano Banana)


**Ease of Use:** very straightforward, clean and simple interface.

**Feature Set:**
- Create multiple clips option
- Create new video option
- Chat to video option
- 50M–500M stock media library (tier-dependent)
- Auto-captions and transcript-based editing
- Brand kits (colors, fonts, logo, watermark) for brand visual consistency on paid plans
- Cloud-based
- Blueprints (train the Lumen5 AI to create videos in a repeatable style, flow and structure)
- Analytics
- RSS feeds (Lumen5 monitors a blog's RSS for new posts and auto-generates videos from them)

Watermark, longest video 2 min

**Business Model:**

## Pictory

**URL:** https://pictory.ai/

**Target Audience:** Marketers, bloggers, content creators and educators repurposing
written content into video.

**Workflow (multiple options):**
1. Text to video
    * Write a script or generate with AI
    * Select brand (optional)
    * Select format (e.g. 1:1)
    * Select scene settings
    * Generate the video and choose a theme (e.g. None, Modern minimalist)
    * Edit the video and save
2. URL to video
    * Scan the URL and AI generates the script
    * Rest of the steps are same as in option 1 (text to video)
3. Audio to video
    * Generates script from audio
    * Rest of the steps are same as in option 1 (text to video)
4. Image to video
5. PPT to video

**Ease of Use:** beginner friendly but it has too many options.

**Feature Set:**
- Script/URL/Audio/Video/Image/PPT-to-video pipeline
- Stock footage from Getty + Storyblocks
- AI voiceover with multiple accents and languages
- Brand kit support
- Projects (group videos into projects)
- Cloud based
- Elevenlabs for AI voice

**Business Model:**

## 4. HeyGen

**URL:** https://heygen.com

**Target Audience:** Marketers, sales teams, L&D professionals, and creators needing spokesperson-style videos.

**Workflow:**
- Choose or create an AI avatar
- Type a script, upload an image/video/PDF/PPT
- Choose options including
    * Prompt idea (AI generates an idea for the video)
    * Video duration (Auto, 15sec, 30sec, 1min)
    * Video orientation (portrait, landscape)
    * Agent mode (chat - chat and plan your video, generate - auto generate your video)
- Agent generates a plan including:
    * Script description (e.g. a passionate and cinematic ad)
    * Style (e.g. bold vibrant)
    * Avatar
    * Voice
    * Detailed scene overview including: scene name (e.g. Intro 0-4s A roll),
    voiceover, visual (e.g. speaking directly to the camera)
- Agent proceeds to make the video (takes significant amount of time)
- Edit in AI studio

**Ease of Use:** Very simple, similar to modern LLM providers

**Feature Set:**
- Best in class AI avatars
- Plan and Generate agent modes available similar to coding agents
- Detailed video plan
- Cloud based
- Voice cloning from short audio samples
- Video translation with lip-sync adjustment
- Script/image/PDF/PPT to avatar video
- Good looking and creative chat options
- Video generation takes a long time

## 5. Runway

**URL:** https://runwayml.com

**Target Audience:** Filmmakers, visual artists, creative directors, and advanced video creators.

**Workflow:**
- Enter a text or image prompt
- Upload frame (required, frame is the first image uploaded that guides the video
creation)
- Choose video options:
    * Duration (5s, 8s or 10s)
    * Preset
    * Format (e.g. 16:9)
    * Model (e.g. Gen-4 Turbo)
- Video generated

**Ease of Use:** Steeper learning curve, motion controls and camera settings require some familiarity with video composition concepts.

**Feature Set:**
- Custom models like GWM-1 and Gen-4.5
- Gen-4 Turbo model (fast, high-quality cinematic clips)
- Text-to-video and image-to-video
- Motion brush, camera controls, keyframing
- Multi-shot consistent characters (Act-Two)
